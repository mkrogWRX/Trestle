Guides & Coaching

Check Back Summer 2017 For Updated Information

Trestle® Bike Park School offers progressive downhill mountain bike programs with very talented and experienced coaches / guides who will coach you on everything from the basics of downhill riding to boosting your skills to take on Trestle's toughest terrain. Using proven methods and techniques, our highly skilled Terramethod certified coaches will advance your riding to the next level. All levels, Intro to Trestle / Trestle 101 through 400, are offered 7 days a week. Call 970.726.1564 for information.

BOOK ONLINE 48+ HOURS IN ADVANCE, or call 800.979.0328.
For minors arriving without a parent or guardian: DOWNLOAD A RELEASE FORM

To ensure that all lessons start on time, please arrive 30 minutes prior to your session.
Groups are formed based on goals and ability levels.
