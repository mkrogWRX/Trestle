Trestle® Bike Shop

Bike Park Shop Map


Why Shop With Us?

Your wardrobe should fit your lifestyle. That’s why the gear inside Trestle Bike Park Shop will speak to your biker soul. Helmets, goggles, pads, shorts, shirts- head to toe, we have got the right fit for you. Browse our selection before heading to get your bike rental. Update your style with comfortable and functional garb that will also look great covered in mud.

Why Rent With Us?

Rent Where You Want to Play

Whether you are staying in town or right at the resort, rent your bike from Trestle® Bike Park Shop and start your biking adventure right. Ride right out of the shop and onto the lift, or the back country trails. No carrying bikes to and from the resort, and no storage hassles.

With over 200 park bikes available for rent and demo, we have the LARGEST selection in the USA. Our bikes are SERVICED NIGHTLY and are custom-fitted to your riding style.

Thanks to our partnerships with Fox and Kali, we are able to provide the complete rental solution for your ride with a complete selection of helmets, padding and armor, all available right at the base. All of our products are brand new, and we have sizing to fit everyone. No other location in Winter Park has the quality and assortment of protection products available to rent and buy.

Trestle® Bike Park Shop is your one-stop shop for all your downhill mountain biking needs.

Reasons to Rent With Us?
  • No Hassle Travel
    Traveling with a bike is inconvenient, expensive and time-consuming. Save the cost and the hassle of bringing your own bike and let us take of everything.
  • The Best, New Gear
    Almost all of our bikes and protection gear are brand new. Our selection is the largest in the USA and includes women's and junior specific park bikes and protection. We have what you want to ride, fresh from the factory.
  • Exclusive Mountainside Location and Free Storage
    We are the only bike shop located at the base of Trestle Bike Park. Free, secure overnight and day storage is included with every rental – meaning you don't have to worry about the security of your equipment or transporting it to and from the resort.
  • Save Money, Reserve your Bike in Advance
    Save up to 10% when prebooking a bike rental and lift pass combo.
  • Peace of Mind
    When renting from us, we have you covered. Bikes can and do break, and in the event that a bike rented from us needs to be serviced, we will fix or swap it immediately. No trip into town, no interruption to the day and no hassle.
  • Inside Knowledge
    Our staff are passionate riders who know the mountain and love to share riding secrets for riders of all ages and ability. No one knows our mountain like we do!

Bike Storage Service

Fast, Convenient Storage – GUARANTEED!

Store your bike right at the base of the mountain, just steps away from the Zephyr Express lift. Trestle® Bike Park Shop's valet storage facility provides a secure location for your bike, and is offered daily from 9am – 6pm.
