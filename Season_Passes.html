Trestle Season Pass Add-On 2016*

All of our season passes are now reloadable. Make sure to bring your winter RFID QuickTrax season pass to the resort to avoid a $5 media fee. The cost to you for re-issuing a new season pass media card will be $5. Should you choose to reuse your season pass media card for future seasons, you will not be charged this fee again. Reasons for you choosing to not reuse your existing season pass media card may include: a name change, a photo update, a lost or destroyed card or the creation of a new Winter Park Account instead of reloading your existing pass. If Winter Park Resort chooses to re-issue you a new season pass media card for any reason not initiated by you, a $5 processing fee will not be charged.
