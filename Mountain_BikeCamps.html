Experience the thrill and excitement of conquering the bike park with one of our Trestle® Bike Park Camps.
Choose from our roster of camps below. Call 970.726.1564 for information, or inquire at Trestle® Bike Park Shop.

Gravity Goddess 2 Day Downhill Camps

July 23-24th or August 20-21st (ages 13+)

Trestle® Bike Park School presents a freeride downhill mountain bike experience exclusively for women. This two-day camp is designed specifically for women, and will cover the best methods for bike control to advance your riding to a whole new level! Using video analysis and proven coaching methods, in small groups of women, the Gravity Goddess Camp ensures that you will get the most out of your downhill riding experience. Channel your inner goddess and add a lot of gravity to your downhill riding – register today! Call 970.726.1564 for more information. Pre-registration is recommended but not required.

BOOK ONLINE 48+ HOURS IN ADVANCE, or call 800.979.0328.
Day-of registration available at Trestle® Bike Shop, located in the Village at Winter Park.
Registration begins at 9am, and the camp runs until 5pm.

Trestle® Freeride 1 Day Camps

July 9th or August 13th

Add freedom to your downhill riding! Come conquer the Trestle® Bike Park in our comprehensive one day camps devoted to transforming you into a downhill dominator. Our top bike park coaches will provide you with the best tips and techniques for hitting features and elevating your downhill riding. Using video analysis and methods perfected by professional downhill riders, at the end of the day you'll be able to ride and jump anywhere on the mountain. You'll be in small groups of people for 6 hours. Call 970.726.1564 for more information. Pre-registration is recommended but not required.

BOOK ONLINE 48+ HOURS IN ADVANCE, or call 800.979.0328.
Day-of registration available at Trestle® Bike Shop, located in the Village at Winter Park. Registration begins at 9am.
